The Best Movies According to Faisal Ahmed

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Godfather (1972) directed by Francis Ford Coppola

The Godfather is a classic American crime film that tells the story of the Corleone family and their involvement in the mafia underworld. Starring Marlon Brando and Al Pacino, the film has won multiple Academy Awards and is considered one of the greatest films of all time.
